Who We Are

Thanjavur's trusted name
in marriage & event décor

RR Decoration Center is a full-service event management company based on Pudukkottai Road, Thanjavur. From the muhurtham mandap to the reception stage, from a child's first birthday to a corporate evening — our team plans, designs and installs every detail so you can simply enjoy the celebration.

We blend traditional Thanjavur grandeur — gold drapes, fresh jasmine, temple-inspired backdrops — with modern lighting, balloon art and theme styling, all delivered on time, every time.

  • On-time, on-budget execution
  • In-house floral & lighting team
  • Custom theme & balloon design
  • Serving Thanjavur & nearby towns
